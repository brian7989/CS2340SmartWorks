About Us

Our Mission

At SmartWorks our goal is to create an affordable and efficient smart home system. we strive to help customers achieve their dream home, fully automated with minimal effort. Our employees focus on optimizing the system and creating a pleasurable experience for all. We hope to deliver affordable and quality software to the public.

Our Story

Our team is filled with aspiring software developers who wanted to take their skills to the next level and develop a satisfactory product for us and you, the customers. We started out in the summer of 2019 as a small group of three students and hope to develop our product even more.
